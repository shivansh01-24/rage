Government Schemes

Golden Fields is dedicated to helping farmers access valuable government schemes designed to support agricultural activities. We provide detailed information on various schemes, including their benefits and application procedures.

Available Schemes

We offer comprehensive details on government schemes that can benefit you in various aspects of farming. This includes:

  • Subsidies: Information on subsidies available for purchasing equipment, seeds, and fertilizers.
  • Loans: Details on low-interest loans to support farm expansion and infrastructure improvements.
  • Grants: Access to grants for research, development, and innovative farming practices.
  • Insurance: Schemes providing insurance coverage for crops and livestock against unforeseen events.

To make the application process easier, we also provide direct links to the relevant government portals where you can apply for these schemes and get more information.
